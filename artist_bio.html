About me

My name is Rebecca Nie, and I have been playing with lines and colors for as long as I can remember. After finishing postsecondary and graduate studies at the University of Toronto and Stanford University, I continue my serious pursuit in traditional art while living and working in Northern California. I strive to portray the impermanent beauty of the subjective reality and the everlasting delicacy of everyday experience through my work.

Exhibitions:

  • 2012, Pacific Art League - Waterworks, Palo Alto CA
  • 2011, Wallenberg Hall - Gallery 160, Stanford University
  • 2012, Student Exhibition at COHO, Stanford University
  • 2009, Smothering Heat, University of Toronto
