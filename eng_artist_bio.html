About me

I have been musing about the lines and colours for as long as I can remember. It is my goal to become a well rounded artist with traditional medias such as drawing and painting, as well as with photography. My work has been show cased in events in the municipal and university community, while my murals hang on walls of public building.  Upon moving to California, I plan to seek more publicity for my art, and I am excited to receive the criticism and blast of ego that comes with it.
